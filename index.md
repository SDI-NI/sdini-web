# Save Democracy Indivisible North Inland

We are neighbors and community members working together to support democracy, civil liberties, equal justice, and the rule of law through peaceful civic participation.

## Welcome

Our chapter brings people together for rallies, vigils, meetings, educational events, and community action. We believe in respectful engagement, informed participation, and building a stronger local network of people who care about democracy.

## What You’ll Find Here

- Upcoming rallies, meetings, and actions
- A mailing list signup form
- Photos from recent events
- Educational and civic resources
- Ways to get involved

## Get Started

- Visit the [Events](/events/) page to see what’s coming up
- Visit [Join Us](/join/) to sign up for announcements
- Browse the [Gallery](/gallery/) for event photos
- Read our [Resources](/resources/) pages for useful information

## Our Values

We support peaceful public action, civic participation, democratic institutions, freedom, equality, and the rights guaranteed to all people.

> Peaceful, visible, informed, and engaged.
