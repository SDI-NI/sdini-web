# About Us

Save Democracy Indivisible North Inland is a community-based chapter for people who want to take part in peaceful, constructive civic engagement.

We gather to:

- defend democratic values
- support the rule of law
- encourage informed civic participation
- build community around freedom, equality, and justice
- provide a welcoming place for people who want to take meaningful action

## What We Do

Our chapter may host or participate in:

- rallies and vigils
- member meetings
- educational gatherings
- volunteer coordination
- outreach and public information efforts

## Who We Welcome

We welcome people who believe in peaceful engagement, democratic participation, and respectful collaboration.
